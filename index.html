Abbie is a recent graduate of Southwest Baptist University, currently working at the Kansas City Repertory Theater.

Along with many shows worked on during college, Abbie has spent summers at Theater West in Nebraska, The Des Moines Metro Opera, and the Kansas City Theater in the Park. She has found her niche in scenic painting but also has experience in design and carpentry.
